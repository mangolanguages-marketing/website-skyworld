Opting for a technology-first approach in uncertain times

In response to school shutdown, Kenny Wolleat implemented Mango Classroom to supplement his distance learning Spanish curriculum, replacing textbooks with technology. After seeing usage soar, he can’t wait to get back to class and hear his students’ progress. 

“I'm excited to get back in the classroom with these kids and actually see... I know there was serious learning going on.”
Kenny Wolleat, Spanish Teacher, Duluth Edison Charter Schools

Duluth Edison Charter Schools is a K-8 public charter school district located in Duluth, Minnesota.
