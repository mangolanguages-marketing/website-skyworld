Centered around your distance learning and homeschool curriculums, Mango’s virtual learning platform keepsstudents on track and parents in the loop on their learner’s progress. Below are the answers to some of our most frequently asked questions.

How does Mango work?

The Mango learning system is powered by linguist-developed methodology that intuitively teaches vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, and culture through everyday conversations recorded by native speakers.

Learning Features

  • The All Languages Subscription allows for a parent profile with up to 5 child profiles
  • Mango automatically tracks each user’s activity/usage time
  • A user’s activity/usage can easily be downloaded as a CSV file
  • Language courses contain automatically graded quizzes/assessments
  • If a student has already made some progress in a language, Mango offers built-in placement tests
  • Mango contains a visually indicative learning pathway to easily see what has been covered and what remains
  • Additional learning activities and Study Reminders to reinforce learned skills and to motivate students
  • Offline learning access for low connectivity environments, plus Bluetooth and auto play features for on-the-go learning
